All about Coffee that you need to know

What actually is Coffee??

Coffee is a popular beverage made from the roasted seeds of the Coffea plant, commonly referred to as coffee beans. It's known for its rich flavor and aroma, as well as its stimulating effects due to caffeine, a natural psychoactive compound found in varying amounts in coffee.

How can I make this beverage?

  1. Grab a mug
  2. Put one teaspoon of instant coffee powder in
  3. Boil water
  4. Add water to the mug
  5. Enjoy!

Different coffee types

Black Coffee
  1. Fill mug completely with water to make black coffee
  2. Stir!
  3. Add sugar if you desire
  4. Stir again!

White Coffee
  1. Fill half of the mug with water
  2. Then fill the other half with milk
  3. Stir!
  4. Add sugar if you desire
  5. Stir again!
